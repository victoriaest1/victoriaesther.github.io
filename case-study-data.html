Key findings

What the model uncovered

23%
Subscription overlap
Nearly a quarter of recurring charges were overlapping services — streaming, cloud storage, and productivity tools with redundant functionality.
3×
Weekend spend spike
Dining and entertainment spend on weekends was 3x higher than weekdays, representing the single largest optimisation opportunity.
15%
Seasonal overspend
November–December spending exceeded budgets by 15–25% consistently. Predictive alerts 4 weeks ahead reduced this to under 8%.
